At Wika Translate we put quality first, and we are very protective when it comes to our reputation. We assign your project to the experienced team of translators in your related field, in order for us to deliver the best services in the industry.

What’s the difference between a certified and a notarized translation?

Certified translation refers to translations done, signed and sealed by a professional translator. Notarized translation refers to official documents thoroughly and accurately translated from the source into the target language which are then authenticated by a public notary. We are always a call away if you happen to not be sure of what type of document you need, Contact us at anytime.

I have a birth certificate that I want translated and certified. Can you provide this service?

Yes, Wika Translate provides certified translations for all official documents, such as Degrees, Transcripts, Police clearance, birth/death certificates, Divorce certificates, celibacy and marriage certificates etc..
